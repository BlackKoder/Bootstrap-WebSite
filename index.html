Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. is a Chinese multinational technology company. Provides telecommunications equipment and sells consumer electronics, smart phones. Its headquarters are located in the city of Shenzhen (Guangdong).

Huawei has deployed its products and services in more than 170 countries. Huawei surpassed Ericsson in 2012 as the world's largest telecommunications equipment maker, and surpassed Apple in 2018 as the world's second-largest smartphone maker, behind Samsung Electronics. In December 2019, Huawei reported that its annual revenue had increased to $ 121.72 billion in 2019.11 In that same year, the company expanded rapidly in Latin America, a region where it has invested heavily in marketing to position the brand since 2014. Huawei it has opened data centers in Chile and Brazil and has expansion plans in Mexico.

Outstanding Features

The Huawei Enjoy 10 Plus is a smartphone belonging to the current lower middle class. This smartphone has been launched by the Chinese manufacturer Huawei in September 2019.
This device is characterized by its generous 6.59-inch screen with Full HD + resolution. It also has a large capacity internal storage, in this case 128 GB, so in this section we are well served. On the other hand, it has a RAM memory that goes from 4 GB for its basic version, 6 GB for the intermediate version and 8 GB for the most premium.

Other features

The Huawei Enjoy 10 Plus is marketed in colors: blue, orange, dark green and black and is a dual SIM smartphone. Regarding the operating system, it comes with Android 9 Pie with the manufacturer's interface EMUI 9.1. It also has a fingerprint sensor located on the back and a 3.5 mm headphone output. Regarding its design, it has rounded edges, curved axes and gradient colors.
